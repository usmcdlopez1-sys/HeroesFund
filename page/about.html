About Heroes Support Fund

Heroes Support Fund is a dedicated nonprofit organization committed to supporting United States military personnel and their families, both those serving overseas in conflict zones and those stationed domestically.

Founded in 2020, we recognized the critical need for immediate, direct assistance to military families facing financial hardships, medical challenges, and emotional struggles that come with military service.

Our Mission

To provide immediate financial assistance, medical support, and mental health resources to US military personnel and their families, ensuring no hero faces their challenges alone.

What We Do

  • Emergency financial assistance for military families in crisis
  • Medical support for wounded warriors and their families
  • Mental health counseling and PTSD support services
  • Educational scholarships for military children
  • Housing assistance for transitioning veterans
  • 24/7 crisis support hotline

Our Partnerships

We work closely with established military support organizations including Army Emergency Relief, Navy-Marine Corps Relief Society, and local veteran service organizations to maximize our impact and avoid duplication of services.

Important Note: Heroes Support Fund is a private nonprofit organization. It is not a part of the Department of Defense or any of its components and has no governmental status. We are an independent 501(c)(3) charitable organization dedicated to supporting military families.
