About Heroes Support Fund

Heroes Support Fund is a dedicated nonprofit organization committed to supporting the United States military personnel and their families, both those serving overseas in conflict zones and those stationed domestically.

Founded in 2020, we recognized the critical need for immediate, direct assistance to military families facing financial hardships, medical challenges, and emotional struggles that come with military service.

Our Mission

We honor sacrifice by empowering lives. Our mission is to provide dedicated financial and logistical support to active-duty service members, veterans, military families, and wounded warriors when they need it most. We believe that when a service member or their family faces hardship, the civilian community has a duty to stand with them. We translate your generosity directly into emergency grants, educational scholarships, and therapeutic services.

Who We Serve

Our effort covers all branches of the U.S. Armed Forces and their families. This includes:

  • Active-Duty Personnel facing sudden financial emergencies while deployed or on base.
  • Veterans transitioning to civilian life who require assistance with housing, job training, or medical expenses not covered by government programs.
  • Military Spouses and Children needing support for education, career development, or mental health services during periods of separation or injury.
  • Wounded, Ill, and Injured Service Members requiring specialized equipment, accessible home modifications, or long-term therapeutic care.

Our Stories and Values

Our Story: Our organization was founded in 2022 by a group of concerned citizens and retired military officers who recognized a gap in support for veterans struggling with PTSD. What started as a small, local effort has grown into a National movement driven by a simple, profound commitment: never leave a hero behind.

Our Values: Integrity: We operate with total transparency, ensuring every dollar is maximized for our beneficiaries.
Respect: We serve our community with the honor and dignity they deserve.
Impact: We focus on measurable results and providing direct, meaningful assistance.

How Your Donations Make an Impact

Important Note: When you donate to HEROESFUND, you are directly funding programs that change lives. Your support provides: $50 could cover the gas for a veteran to attend a week of critical job interviews. $500 could provide an emergency grant to a military family to keep their utilities on while they await government assistance. $5,000 could fund a full scholarship for a military spouse to complete a certification program, leading to a stable career.
