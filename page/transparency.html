Financial Transparency

Our Commitment

We believe in complete transparency about how your donations are used. Every dollar donated goes directly to supporting military families, with minimal administrative costs.

95% Direct Program Funding

95 cents of every dollar donated goes directly to programs supporting military families. Only 5% goes to administrative costs, which is well below the industry standard of 15-25%.
